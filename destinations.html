Uganda

Uganda is an emerald, undulating and raised destination with many lakes and mountains. Uganda’s appeal falls in its straightforwardness. Infrastructure is limited but growing, and there is now a good range of safari accommodations and city-based hotels.

Rwanda

This is Africa’s serene and most nearby, gorilla trekking is the main draw card of a Rwandan safari. After arriving at the capital city of Kigali, you are only a few hours drive away from the mythical Volcanoes National Park and its familiar but still wild gorilla families.

Kenya

Experience a safari vacation in Kenya, the place where safari travel originated. The best Kenya tours and safaris include Big 5 game viewing, natural beauty, cultural encounters, and the tropical beaches of the Kenyan coast.

Tanzania

Tanzania is well known for its diversity of wildlife, cultures and landscapes. From the classic savannah destinations of the Serengeti, Tarangire and Ngorongoro Crater to the beaches and coral reefs of Zanzibar and the tropical coast, a Tanzania safari holiday delivers one massive experience after another.
